FAQ

What's the difference between packages and credits? ↓
Packages are fixed bundles for a single launch — you pick one and go. Credits are flexible tokens you can use across multiple projects whenever you want. Both include the same platform submissions at the same price.
Do credits expire? ↓
Never. Credits stay in your account until you use them. Buy a Builder Pack today, use it for your next three launches over the next year. No pressure.
Which platforms are included? ↓
80+ platforms across launch sites (Product Hunt, Uneed, DevHunt, BetaList), AI directories (Futurepedia, There's an AI for That), SaaS directories (SaaSHub, AlternativeTo), and communities. Full list on the Platforms page.
What if a platform rejects my submission? ↓
We don't refund for platform rejections — that's outside our control. But we do retry automatically, and our approval rate is 70%+. If our bot fails technically (not a rejection), we re-run it for free.
How fast does it launch? ↓
Immediately. Once you click launch, submissions start queuing. Tier 1 platforms go first (Product Hunt, Uneed, DevHunt). Directories follow over the next 24–48 hours for natural SEO pacing.
Can I use this for multiple projects? ↓
Yes. You can have unlimited projects in your account. Credits work across all of them. If you ship regularly, the Builder Pack at $39 for 200 credits is the best deal.
