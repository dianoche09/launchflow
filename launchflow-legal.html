PRIVACY
POLICY

We take privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Short version: We collect only what's needed to run the service. We don't sell your data. Ever.

1. What We Collect

Account data: Email, name, and OAuth tokens (GitHub/Google) when you sign up.

Project data: Product names, URLs, descriptions, logos, and screenshots you provide for submissions.

Payment data: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

Usage data: Submission logs, platform responses, dashboard activity. Used to run the service and debug issues.

Bot data: Screenshots taken during automated submissions for verification purposes. Stored temporarily.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To submit your product to platforms you select
  • To generate AI launch copy using your product information
  • To send transactional emails (launch started, completed, low credits)
  • To improve our bot automation and submission success rates
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our terms

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (EU/US data centers)
  • OpenAI — AI content generation (your project data is sent to generate copy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy)
  • Upstash — Redis queue for submission jobs
  • Railway — worker hosting
  • Vercel — frontend hosting

When you use LaunchFlow to submit to third-party platforms, your product data (name, URL, description) is sent to those platforms as part of the submission process.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active
  • Project and submission data: retained indefinitely (your launch history)
  • Bot screenshots: deleted after 30 days
  • System logs: deleted after 90 days

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you — email us and we'll send a full export
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Correct inaccurate data via your dashboard
  • Opt out of non-transactional emails

For GDPR requests or privacy concerns: privacy@launchflow.io

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies only: authentication session cookies (Supabase) and basic analytics. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.
