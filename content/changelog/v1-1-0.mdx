The ecosystem moves fast, and so do we. **LaunchFlow v1.1.0** expands your reach deeper into the startup community.

### New Features

- **Guided Submissions**: Platforms like Product Hunt and Reddit have strict anti-bot policies. LaunchFlow now generates your custom copy and gives you a step-by-step UI to manually post in under 2 minutes, remaining 100% compliant.
- **30 New AI & SaaS Directories**: Added Tier 2 platforms including There's an AI for That, Futurepedia, and SaaSHub. Total platform coverage is now 80+.
- **Shareable Launch Reports**: Once a launch completes, you can generate a public summary link or PDF to share your momentum on Twitter/X.

### Bug Fixes & Improvements

- Fixed an issue where the AI launch kit would sometimes hallucinate irrelevant features.
- Improved the automation retry logic. If a platform is temporarily down, the worker will use exponential backoff up to 3 times before failing the job.
