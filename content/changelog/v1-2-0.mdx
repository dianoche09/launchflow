The biggest update since V1 is here. **LaunchFlow v1.2.0** introduces features designed for heavy shippers and B2B marketers.

### New Features

- **Agency / Team Accounts**: You can now invite up to 10 team members to a single LaunchFlow workspace. Share credits, collaborate on copy, and manage multiple client projects seamlessly.
- **AI Platform Matching**: Not every product belongs on every directory. We integrated **Pinecone Vector DB** to semantically match your product's features against our database of 150+ directories. The bots now intelligently skip platforms that aren't a good fit (e.g., skipping a B2B SaaS directory if your product is a consumer mobile app), increasing overall approval rates.

### Dashboard Upgrades

- **Advanced Filtering**: Added comprehensive tagging and filtering to the Projects page.
- **Magic Link Redesign**: The login flow has been overhauled. It's now 100% passwordless via Supabase Magic Links, drastically reducing login friction.

Keep shipping.
