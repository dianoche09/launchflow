# Nailing Your Product Hunt Launch

Product Hunt has changed. The days of simply posting a link and hoping for the best are gone. Today, launching requires strategy, community, and timing.

## 1. The Pre-Launch Checklist

Before you hit submit, make sure you have:

- A compelling tagline (under 60 characters)
- A high-contrast, attention-grabbing logo GIF
- 3-5 crisp screenshots with clear annotations
- A 1st comment that tells your founding story

## 2. Timing is Everything

Always launch at exactly 12:01 AM PST. This gives you the full 24 hours to accumulate upvotes and momentum. If you launch later in the day, you're competing with products that have a head start.

## 3. The Power of "Build in Public"

The most successful launches are from makers who have been sharing their journey for weeks or months. Your Twitter/X audience, your email list, and your early beta testers are your core launch team.

> Don't ask for upvotes. Ask for support, feedback, and engagement. Product Hunt's algorithm penalizes direct vote solicitation.

## Will you get #1?

Maybe, maybe not. But a top 5 finish still drives significant traffic and domain authority. Keep building, keep shipping.
